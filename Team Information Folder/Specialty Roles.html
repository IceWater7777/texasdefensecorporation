*Minor Policy*

-- Anyone ages 12 or older may be recruited to TDC. --

Anyone under 18 will NOT be allowed to join TDC without being accompanied by their parent/guardian. Both parent/guardian and child must be active airsoft players and active members according to their status in TDC.

Anyone under 18 MAY ONLY be invited to join TDC, IF their parent/guardian are already members of TDC. (The invited child must follow the same guidelines as if they were being recruited as an adult.)

Anyone under 18 WILL be removed from TDC if their parent/guardian is also removed. (either by themselves or staff)

These guys represent a relationship between TDC and their team.





*Active Duty*

--TDC Salutes our armed forces personnel.--

To show its appreciation for its members who are Active Duty Military Personnel, Texas Defense Corporation has set forth the following guidelines exception to accommodate the Active Duty TDC member:

All Active Duty Members will be given a special role to signify their active duty status.

Active Duty Members will NOT be required to attend events of any kind.

(They have limited free time and can attend events that line up with their schedules whenever they wish.

Active Duty Members are only asked to check in once a month, and NOT required to check in.

Active Duty Members will only be considered for removal from the team if they haven’t “checked in” after missing 52 weeks.
